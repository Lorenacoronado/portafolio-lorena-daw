About Me

I am actively seeking my first professional experience to apply theory, learn maximally, and consolidate a solid foundation that allows me to grow long-term in this fascinating world.

My passion for computing is vocational and began at the age of 14, exploring web design (blogs, Photoshop, self-taught creation). This curiosity led me to the Middle Level SMR (Systems, Hardware, and Networks) degree, which consolidated my technical base and confirmed my path towards web development.

My biggest differentiator is my work experience in other areas.
